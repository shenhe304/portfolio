The sign up page of meetup international friends -Daily UI-

Prompt

Create a sign up page, modal, form, or app screen related to signing up for something.

GOAL

Participating in various meetups since coming to Canada, so I've decided to create a signup page for a meetup.

Meetups are attended by people of various races and nationalities with the purpose of making friends, so I took care to ensure that it doesn't look like a dating app. The signup page features group photos with both men and women, taken around noon rather than in the evening, to convey an atmosphere of making friends rather than dating.

Details

Signing up on popular SNS platforms
Signing up on popular SNS platforms can be cumbersome, and to avoid dropout due to the hassle of entering information, we have enabled sign-up using the top-used SNS platforms. This allows for a more convenient and seamless sign-up process.
reference:23 Top Social Media Sites to Consider for Your Brand in 2024
Color Settings:
Chosen a bright and vibrant atmosphere, using the color orange (#f7a404) to ensure it stands out and doesn't overlap with common SNS icons.
