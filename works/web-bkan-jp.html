What is
the B Hepatitis Compensation Payment?

In Japan, compensation is provided by the government under the following conditions:

  • Born between July 2, 1941, and January 27, 1988.
  • Sustained infection with the Hepatitis B virus.
  • Received group vaccinations or Tuberculin reaction tests before turning 7 years old.
  • No other infection causes besides group vaccinations, etc.

The identification of eligible recipients for compensation is determined through legal procedures, such as court-mediated settlements. If you engage a law firm to assist you in receiving compensation from the government, after preparing the necessary documents, you can expect to receive compensation from the government.

About the Modal Window

Our law firm has a check page to confirm eligibility for Hepatitis B Allowance. About half of those who reach that page inquire about it, but there are also people who leave without reaching that page.

Pros and Cons

Placement

  • Placed on the column page titled "Regarding Eligibility for Allowance."
  • Set to appear when the user has progressed one-third into the page.

Reasons

  • To prevent abandonment caused by the modal.
  • Anticipating that those who have read one-third are interested in the Hepatitis B Allowance with a probability of around 30-40%.

Measures to prevent page abandonment

To prevent users from leaving the site when a modal window appears, strategically place a prominently sized 'Close' button to avoid surprise exits.
