Designed a modal for a service related to Hepatitis B, where eligible individuals can receive financial assistance from the government. Many people are unsure whether they qualify or not. GOAL

To boost the engagement rate within the site and move closer to conversions, we have implemented a modal that directs users to a page where they can assess whether they are eligible for financial assistance. This modal appears on frequently visited pages.

Details

A color setting that doesn't clash with the page's primary color.
To complement the primary color, which is green (#759e65), on the page, we opted for #f77e6a to be used in the modal for a vibrant contrast.
Design with a Casual Feel
By incorporating a photo of a gently smiling female lawyer, we aimed to convey an image that makes it easy for users to casually check whether they are eligible for financial assistance.
