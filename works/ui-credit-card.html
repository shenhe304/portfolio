Payment of online javascript school -Daily UI-

Prompt

Credit Card Checkout: Design a credit card checkout form or page. Don't forget the important elements such as the numbers, dates, security numbers, etc.

GOAL

To create a payment page with a credit card, it's crucial to provide clarity on what the payment is for and the exact amount. This ensures a sense of reassurance and trust for the user.

Drawing from personal experience, there are instances where, upon reaching the credit card input screen, users may forget the exact payment amount. To address this, I included the payment amount on the input screen, enhancing the user's peace of mind and confidence in the transaction.

Details

Color Settings:
For this online JavaScript course, I opted for a dark theme. Pure black (#000000) can make white text too stark, causing some discomfort and difficulty in readability for certain individuals. Therefore, I chose a softer black (#333333) to create a more comfortable visual experience.
