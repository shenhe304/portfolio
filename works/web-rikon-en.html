ABOUT THE PROJECT

About the Color

Blue / Naivy
Trust, Dependability, Logic
Why I choose the deep blue
Unlike Japan, associating pink with femininity in some overseas cultures can be perceived as discriminatory. Therefore, we chose the main color based on the meanings it carries rather than gender stereotypes.

Details

Main Visual
Placed the words 'Divorce, Children, Money' prominently at the center to draw attention. Positioned an image on the background to convey the idea of divorce through a third party.
Small Message
Since it's a landing page, I wanted visitors to read the page like a story, with their eyes naturally flowing downward. Therefore, I separated 'We are here to support you' from the main visual for emphasis.
Emphasis Zone
"Please leave it to us," reiterating the message.
Harvest Section
Set up a Call-to-Action (CTA), providing both phone and email contact details for inquiries.
Review Section
Japanese people tend to highly value reviews and actual experiences, so I'm creating a section for that.

Responsive Design

Have considered coding and created designs for both PC and mobile (SP). I've verified them on actual devices, adjusting for the readability of text and ensuring there are no design disruptions

English Design

Translating and redesigning work done in Japan into English, giving it a Western-style design.
